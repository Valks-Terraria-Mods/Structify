Tired of building structures? Let this mod do it for you!

Recipes (Crafted at a Heavy Workbench)
- Small House (50 wood)
- Medium House (100 wood)
- Large House (175 wood, 25 stone)
- Tower Cluster (200 wood)
- Castle Gate (150 stone)
- Fishing Pond (150 stone)
- Hellevator (100 stone)

Planned

- Greenhouse
- Remastering and Tweaking Existing Structures
