Tired of building structures? Let this mod do it for you!

Recipes (Crafted at Heavy Workbench)
- Small Underground House (50 wood)
- Small House (50 wood)
- Bridge (50 stone)
- Medium House (100 wood)
- Large House (175 wood, 25 stone)
- Prison Cluster (200 wood)
- Hellevator (100 stone)
