Tired of building structures? Let this mod do it for you!

A new NPC named Bob will move into your town and sell you the structures.

Current Structures
- Small House Type 1
- Small House Type 2
- Large House
- Greenhouse Type 1
- Greenhouse Type 2
- Fishing Pond
- Tower Gate
- Tower Cluster
- Large Outdoors Boss Arena
- Hellavator
