While holding a structure item, click anywhere in the world to place a house.

Recipes (all made at a heavy workbench)
- Small Underground House (50 wood)
- Small House (50 wood)
- Bridge (50 stone)
- Medium House (100 wood)
- Large House (175 wood, 25 stone)
- Prison Cluster (200 wood)
